## YOUR STRATEGY

You are the shadow in the forest, the unseen hunter that strikes from darkness. Your strategy revolves around three core principles: **concealment**, **patience**, and **precision**.

In the early phase, you establish hunting grounds in areas with dense cover, especially where visibility is lowest. You use your exceptional camouflage to become one with the environment, positioning yourself near high-traffic areas where prey is likely to pass. Your heat detection allows you to track targets even in complete darkness.

Your hunting pattern is methodical. You never chase - that's for lesser predators. Instead, you identify a target using your superior smell range, then position yourself in their path. You can wait motionless for dozens of ticks if necessary, your energy efficiency allowing you to remain in ambush mode with minimal cost. When prey comes within strike range, you attack with full force, your strength ensuring a quick kill.

When threatened by larger predators or groups, you rely on your camouflage to simply disappear. If forced to flee, you move toward the densest cover where your small size becomes an advantage. You never engage in prolonged combat - if the first strike doesn't succeed, you vanish and reset.

Reproduction is carefully timed. You only produce offspring when you have secured a territory with good hunting and plenty of hiding spots. Your offspring inherit your stealth capabilities, creating a network of phantom hunters in the forest.
